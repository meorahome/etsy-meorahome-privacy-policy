Privacy Policy for Meora Home Manager

Last updated: February 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes the policies and procedures regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when using the Meora Home Manager application ("the App") and informs You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You.

Interpretation and Definitions

Interpretation

Words with capitalized initial letters have meanings defined under the following conditions. These definitions apply regardless of whether they appear in singular or plural form.

Definitions

  • App (Application): Refers to Meora Home Manager, a personal content management tool used for preparing and publishing content related to the Meora Home Etsy shop.
  • Operator: Refers to Meora Home (referred to as "We", "Us", or "Our" in this Policy), the sole operator and user of the App.
  • Third-Party Services: External platforms and APIs integrated with the App, including Google Drive, Google Sheets, and Pinterest API.
  • Personal Data: Any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
  • Processing: Any operation performed on data, including collection, storage, modification, transfer, or deletion.
  • You/User: Any individual whose data may be processed through the App's operations, including visitors to content published via the App.

Purpose of the App

Meora Home Manager is a personal content management tool operated solely by Meora Home. The App assists the Operator in preparing and managing content for the Meora Home Etsy shop Pinterest account.

The App's workflow includes:

  • Organizing and accessing product images stored in the Operator's Google Drive
  • Managing product listing data via the Operator's Google Sheets
  • Preparing content drafts (pin descriptions, titles, and images) for Pinterest
The Operator manually reviews, selects, and approves each individual pin before it is published to Pinterest. No content is published without the Operator's explicit action and approval. The App does not perform any bulk or unsupervised publishing.

The App is not publicly available, has no end users, and does not provide services to external parties. It is used exclusively by the Operator for managing their own Pinterest business account.

Data Collection and Processing

Data Processed Through Third-Party Services

The App interacts with the following third-party services through their official APIs:

  • Google Drive: Used to access and retrieve product images stored in the Operator's own Google Drive account.
  • Google Sheets: Used to read and manage product listing data maintained by the Operator.
  • Pinterest API: Used to publish pins to the Operator's own Pinterest business account. Each pin is individually reviewed and approved by the Operator before publishing.

Types of Data Processed

The App may process the following types of data:

  • Product images and descriptions from the Operator's own accounts
  • Product listing metadata (titles, tags, prices) from the Operator's Google Sheets
  • Pinterest account data necessary for content publishing

Data Not Collected

The App does not:

  • Collect personal data from end users or website visitors
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Collect usage data, IP addresses, or device information from third parties
  • Store or process data of children or any individuals under the age of 13
  • Require user registration or account creation by external users
  • Access or process data from any Pinterest accounts other than the Operator's own account

Data Storage and Retention

The App utilizes n8n as a workflow management platform to assist the Operator with content preparation tasks. The App does not maintain a separate database or persistent data storage. Data handled by the App resides within the Operator's existing accounts on Google Drive, Google Sheets, and Pinterest.

Temporary data processed during workflow execution is not retained after the workflow completes.

Third-Party Services

The App relies on third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Pinterest Privacy Policy: https://policy.pinterest.com/en/privacy-policy
  • n8n Privacy Policy: https://n8n.io/legal/privacy

We encourage You to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data processed through the App, including:

  • Using official APIs with proper authentication (OAuth 2.0) for all third-party service connections
  • Limiting access to the App to the Operator only
  • Not sharing processed data with any unauthorized third parties
  • Keeping all API access credentials private and secure

Compliance with Pinterest Developer Guidelines

The App is designed and operated in compliance with Pinterest Developer Guidelines:

  • The Operator manually reviews and approves each pin before publishing
  • No actions are taken on Pinterest without the Operator's explicit initiation
  • The App does not access any third-party Pinterest accounts
  • The App does not scrape, store, or redistribute any Pinterest data
  • The App is not used for advertising, audience targeting, or data collection purposes

Your Rights

If You believe that any of Your personal data has been inadvertently processed through the App, You may contact Us to request access, correction, or deletion of such data.
